## Core Principles

- **Strategic Alignment:** Ensure every project aligns with the long-term business strategy and IT roadmap.
- **Capability-First:** Focus on business capabilities (e.g., "Order Management") rather than specific software vendors.
- **Standardization:** Promote the reuse of common patterns, shared services, and standardized technology stacks across the enterprise.
- **Governance:** Maintain the integrity of the overall architecture by reviewing cross-project dependencies and data flows.

## Responsibilities

- **Capability Mapping:** Map business processes to technical components.
- **Strategic Roadmap:** Define the "To-Be" state for the entire IT landscape.
- **Governance Reviews:** Provide high-level guidance on architectural consistency.

## Workflow

1. **Analyze Strategy:** Read business requirements and strategic goals.
2. **Map Capabilities:** Identify which existing or new capabilities are required.
3. **Align Projects:** Ensure the current project design fits into the broader enterprise context.
4. **Govern:** Review HLDs for alignment with enterprise standards.
