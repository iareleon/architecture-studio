# Social Media Guide

Platform setup and posting cadence reference for content creators. All examples use
placeholder brand names — replace with your own brand in each step.

---

## Platform Setup Checklist

### LinkedIn

1. Company page: name, logo (300×300 px), banner (1128×191 px), tagline, website URL
2. Personal page (founder/creator): headline, summary, featured section for pinned posts
3. Connect company and personal pages via "Your company" on the personal profile

### X (Twitter)

1. Handle: `@YourBrandName` — keep it short and identical across platforms where available
2. Profile photo: 400×400 px (square, renders as circle)
3. Header: 1500×500 px
4. Bio: 160 characters — include primary keyword and CTA (e.g. `🔗 link in bio`)
5. Pinned tweet: your highest-value post or announcement

### Instagram

1. Switch to a Business or Creator account (Settings → Account → Switch to professional)
2. Profile photo: 320×320 px
3. Bio: 150 characters — emojis work well as separators
4. Link in bio: use a link-in-bio page if linking to multiple destinations
5. Story Highlights: create permanent highlight covers (use consistent branded icons)

### YouTube

1. Channel name: matches brand name
2. Profile photo: 800×800 px
3. Channel art (banner): 2560×1440 px (safe area for all devices: 1235×338 px centre)
4. Channel description: 1000 characters — include primary keywords in the first 100
5. About section: website, email for business enquiries, social links
6. Upload default settings: set default category, default tags

## Posting Cadence (Starting Recommendations)

| Platform | Frequency | Best times (local) |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | 3–5× / week | Tue–Thu 8–10am, 12pm |
| X / Twitter | 1–3× / day | Mon–Fri 9am, 12pm, 5–6pm |
| Instagram feed | 3–5× / week | Mon–Fri 11am–1pm, 7–9pm |
| Instagram stories | Daily | Morning + evening |
| YouTube | 1–2× / week | Thu–Fri 2–4pm |

> Cadence should reflect your publishing capacity. Consistency matters more than frequency.
> Review analytics after 30 days and adjust.

## Cross-Platform Repurposing Map

| Source | Repurpose as |
|---|---|
| Long-form video (YouTube) | 3–5 Shorts/Reels clips, 1 LinkedIn post, 3–5 X tweets |
| LinkedIn article | 5–7 X threads, 1 Instagram carousel |
| X thread | 1 LinkedIn post, 1 Instagram carousel (thread as slides) |
| Blog post | 1 LinkedIn article, 1 YouTube explainer |

---

## Content Mix (80/20 Rule)

- **80%** value-first: educational, entertaining, community-building content
- **20%** promotional: product/service mentions, CTAs, launches

---

## Caption Structure

1. Hook (first line — must stop the scroll)
2. Body (2–4 short paragraphs or bullets — one idea per paragraph)
3. CTA (single clear ask: comment, share, click link, follow)
4. Hashtags (LinkedIn: 3–5 / X: 1–2 / Instagram: 10–20)
